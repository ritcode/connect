Features

Unlimited
There is neither a limit on number of participants who can join a room or any time limit.
Private chats
You can chat with either everyone or an individual in the room during the call.
Decentralised
There is no central server through which your stream or chats are being trasmitted, rather its peer-to-peer but there is a server up and running merely for signaling purpose.
Whiteboard
This platform contains a minimalist whiteboard for more interesting and interactive calls.
Recording
You can record your calls for future references.
